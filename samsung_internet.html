Samsung Internet

Samsung’s browser for Gear VR

Download Version 4.2.00-14 298.85 MB

Instructions

  1. Search for and install Samsung Internet Browser from the Oculus Gear VR store.
  2. Launch Samsung Internet Browser from your Gear VR Library.
  3. Navigate to a WebVR-compatible site.
  4. Accept the option to enable WebVR.
  5. Enjoy!
